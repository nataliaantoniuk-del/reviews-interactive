The anatomy of a 5-star session

This is your checklist — every single session.

1

Warm start

The client feels your presence immediately. Not just "Hi" — but a signal that you're fully there for them.

2

Empathy first

Acknowledge what they're feeling before you answer anything. "I hear you" before "here's what I see."

3

Context check

Ask 1–2 precise questions before diving in. An answer that misses the point can't earn 5 stars.

4

Clear, specific answer

No filler, no vague generalities. The client should understand the "what" and the "why."

5

Practical next step

End with 1–3 concrete things they can do. A client who leaves with a plan feels value.

6

Warm close

Don't just stop. End with warmth and an invitation to return. The last thing they feel shapes their review.

REMEMBER

The critical threshold is ≥ 90% of reviews rated 4–5 stars. If it drops below that — it's a red flag that needs immediate attention.
